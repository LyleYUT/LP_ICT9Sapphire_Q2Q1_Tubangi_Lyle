Crypto Currency Research

The following presents a brief research on three famous Crypto Currencies in Philippine value.

Bitcoin is a cryptocurrency and a blockchain. Bitcoin mining is the race between miners to hash specific values and other block information in order to find the solution to a hashing problem and add a block to the blockchain. After that, the winning miner is rewarded with bitcoins.

One Bitcoin is equal to 3,974,295 PHP

Dogecoin was created as an alternative to traditional cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin. The name and Shiba Inu logo are based on a meme. Unlike Bitcoin, Dogecoin is intentionally abundant — with 10,000 new coins mined every minute and no maximum supply.

One DOGE coin is equal to 9.5 PHP

Shiba Inu cryptocurrency features the Shiba Inu—a Japanese breed of hunting dog—as its mascot. Shiba Inu is widely considered to be an alternative to Dogecoin; in fact, proponents of Shiba Inu have touted it in the past as "the Dogecoin killer."

One Shiba Inu coin is equal to 0.001013 PHP
